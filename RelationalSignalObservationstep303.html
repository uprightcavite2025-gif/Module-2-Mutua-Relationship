Step 3 — Record Your Observations
Reflection
At the end of the observation period, take a few minutes to reflect on your experience.
You will be asked to answer the following questions.
⚠ Important
Please download the Relational Signal Observation Worksheet before starting this step so you can record your reflections clearly.
Reflect on the following
  • Which relational signal did you practice this week?
  • In which situation did you consciously apply it?
  • What did you observe in the interaction?
  • What felt easier than expected?
  • What remained challenging?
Your responses do not need to be long.
Focus on specific situations from real conversations.
Reflection strengthens behavioral learning because it connects experience with awareness (Kolb, 1984).
